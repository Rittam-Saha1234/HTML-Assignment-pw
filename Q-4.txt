Q. What is the purpose of the <meta> tag in HTML? How is it commonly used?
Ans. The <meta> tag provides metadata about the HTML document. 

    It is commonly used for:
        * Specifying character encoding: <meta charset="UTF-8">
        * Defining viewport settings: <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
        * SEO purposes: <meta name="description" content="Webpage description">
